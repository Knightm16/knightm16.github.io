Minecraft Earth Server Project

Playing Minecraft back in early beta, I always lamented the boring world gen and lack of life to all of my builds. To help make towns and nations feel alive, I developed a system to fill out settlements with NPCs. This developed into a game, as different villagers needed different requirements, from food, to amenities, to small supply chains. Soon the world was becoming more an more populated. I moved the idea to an earth map for more interesting geography, and then began adding new blocks and items to make different parts of Earth feel more unique!


As the server developed, it became a wonderful project, in which those who liked the idea continued to join! If you think this is your kind of game, come join us at the discord link below!
